Avenir is a geometric sans serif inspired by Future and Erbar. Comprised of varied line weights, Avenir is Frutiger's humanistic reinterpretation of modern type. Clean yet versatile, it has been increasingly used by contemporary designers. French for "future", Avenir has truly lived up to its name as numerous comapnies have utilized it in updatating their digital presence.

Adrian Frutiger 1988
